Orientation

Beginners should start here. The MUN Handbook and Orientation PPT will introduce you to the concept of a MUN.

Position Papers

This features position papers from different committees. This will be useful to gauge the basic format of a position paper.

Resolutions

This has resolutions from different committees. It is useful to determine the format of clauses.

About

  • June 2017

    The First Ever BGSMUN

    The first cohort of BGS MUNners got together to conduct the first MUN in this school. This intra-school MUN served to train the next cohort. 

  • June 2019

    BGSMUN'19

    After a one-year hiatus, recognizing the need to train a new set of students; we conducted the second BGSMUN.

  • July 2019

    Best Delegation at DiPSMUN'19

    After many misses and the passing out of BGS's finest MUNner yet, we won the best delegation award at DiPSMUN'19 conducted by DPS Bangalore South.
